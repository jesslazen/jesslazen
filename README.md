I am a social science researcher with an M.S. in Applied Statistics from NYU. I focus on humanizing data using quantitative and qualitative methods in R and Python. My work blends advanced statistical modeling with narrative insight to better understand human behavior and social systems.

I have experience leading large-scale research projects and analyzing behavioral, linguistic, and attitudinal data. I also teach and mentor students across NYU, CUNY, and NYC public schools, where I emphasize data literacy and ethics.
